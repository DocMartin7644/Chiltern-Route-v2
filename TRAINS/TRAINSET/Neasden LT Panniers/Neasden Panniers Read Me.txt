This is a pack of six LT GWR 57xx class Pannier Tank that were believed to be based at Neasden LT depot. 

They are reskins of Frank Sandwell's GWR 57xx Pannier Tank and Frank also assisted with applying the LT logo and numbers. 
Many thanks go to Frank for his assistance and permission to produce this pack.

Thanks also go to Lew Price for permission to include his GWR Cab.

The GWR Sound set (File ID: 6481) and the steam crew (File ID: 7706) are also required.

The numbers are as follow :-
 

L89
L90
L94
L95
L97
L99

The first three have Automatic Coupling and the last three Chain.

To install just run the program supplied.
