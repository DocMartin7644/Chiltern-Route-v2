Cabview for UK Class60 Diesel Locomotive.

Install:
 1 Backup and delete the oroginal folder of [CABVIEW].
 2 Unzip [60cabV2.zip] and put the folder of [CABVIEW]
   into your trainset(ex. [CLASS60100],[60004coal]).
 3 Change next line of eng-file.

   CabView ( xxxxxxxx.cvf ) ----->  CabView ( class60.cvf )

-------------------------------------------------------------

If you use common.cab

 1 Unzip [60cabV2.zip] and rename [CABVIEW] to [60cab].
 2 Put [60cab] into the folder of [common.cab].
 3 Put [class60C.cvf] into the folder of [CABVIEW] of your trainset.
 4 Change next line of eng-file.

   CabView ( class60.cvf ) ----->  CabView ( class60C.cvf )
