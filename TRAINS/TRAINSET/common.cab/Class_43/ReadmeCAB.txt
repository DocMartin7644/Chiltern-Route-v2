Cabview for UK Class43(HST) Diesel Locomotive.

-------------------------------------------------------------
Install:
 1 Backup and delete an oroginal folder of [CABVIEW].
 2 Unzip [HSTcabV2.zip] and put the folder of [CABVIEW]
   into your trainset(ex.[Virgin_Trains_HST_125],[Ex_Virgin_43155]...).
 3 Backup and delete an original eng-file, and put eng-files of the
  folder of [Virgin_Trains_HST_125_eng-file] or [Ex_Virgin_43155_eng-file] into your trainset.

 * If you use another trainset, you must change next line of eng-file.
   CabView ( xxxx.cvf ) ----->  CabView ( hstcab.cvf )
   

-------------------------------------------------------------

   If you use common.cab

 1 Unzip [HSTcabV2.zip] and rename [CABVIEW] to [HSTcab].
 2 Put [HSTcab] into the folder of [common.cab].
 3 Put [hstcabC.cvf] into the folder of [CABVIEW] of your trainset.
 4 Backup and delete an original eng-file, and put eng-files of the
  folder of [Virgin_Trains_HST_125_eng-file] or [Ex_Virgin_43155_eng-file] into your trainsets.
 5 Change next line of eng-file.
   CabView ( hstcab.cvf ) ----->  CabView ( hstcabC.cvf )


 * If you use another trainset, you must change next line of eng-file.
   CabView ( xxxx.cvf ) ----->  CabView ( hstcabC.cvf )
