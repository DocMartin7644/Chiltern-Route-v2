Cabview for UK Class33(Crompton) Diesel locomotive.


-------------------------------------------------------------
Install:
 1 Backup and delete the oroginal folder of [CABVIEW].
 2 Unzip [33cabV2.zip] and put the folder of [CABVIEW]
   into your trainset(ex.[IJ_33041_065_blue], [33103fragonset]...).
 3 Change next line of eng-file.

 (1)

   CabView ( xxxx.cvf ) ----->  CabView ( class33.cvf )
