Cabview for UK Class180(Adelante) Diesel Multiple Unit.

Install:
 1 Backup and delete the oroginal folder of [CABVIEW].
 2 Unzip [180cabV1.zip] and put the folder of [CABVIEW]
   into your trainset([Class 180]).
 3 Backup and delete an original eng-file, and put eng-files of the
  folder of [Class 180_eng-file] into your trainsets.

 * If you use another trainset, you must change next line of eng-file.
   CabView ( xxxx.cvf ) ----->  CabView ( class180.cvf )
   

-------------------------------------------------------------

   If you use common.cab

 1 Unzip [180cabV1.zip] and rename [CABVIEW] to [180cab].
 2 Put [180cab] into the folder of [common.cab].
 3 Put [class180C.cvf] into the folder of [CABVIEW] of your trainset.
 4 Backup and delete an original eng-file, and put eng-files of the
  folder of [Class 180] into your trainsets.
 5 Change next line of eng-file.
   CabView ( class180.cvf ) ----->  CabView ( class180C.cvf )
