Cabview for UK Class66 Diesel locomotive.

-------------------------------------------------------------
Install:
 1 Backup and delete the oroginal folder of [CABVIEW].
 2 Unzip [66cabV1.zip] and put the folder of [CABVIEW]
   into your trainset(ex.[MCLW_66][JT42CWR_66609]...).

-------------------------------------------------------------

If you use common.cab

 1 Unzip [66cabV1.zip] and rename [CABVIEW] to [66cab].
 2 Put [66cab] into the folder of [common.cab].
 3 Put [class66C.cvf] into the folder of [CABVIEW] of your trainset.
 4 Change next line of eng-file.

   CabView ( class66.cvf ) ----->  CabView ( class66C.cvf )
