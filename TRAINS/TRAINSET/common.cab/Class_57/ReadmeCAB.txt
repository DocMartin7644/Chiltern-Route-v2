Cabview for UK Class57 Diesel locomotive.

-------------------------------------------------------------
Install:
 1 Backup and delete the oroginal folder of [CABVIEW].
 2 Unzip [57cabV1.zip] and put the folder of [CABVIEW]
   into your trainset(ex.[57012][Freightliner_57001]...).
 3 Change next line of eng-file.

   CabView ( xxxxxxx.cvf ) ----->  CabView ( class57.cvf )

-------------------------------------------------------------

If you use common.cab

 1 Unzip [57cabV1.zip] and rename [CABVIEW] to [57cab].
 2 Put [57cab] into the folder of [common.cab].
 3 Put [class57C.cvf] into the folder of [CABVIEW] of your trainset.
 4 Change next line of eng-file.

   CabView ( class57.cvf ) ----->  CabView ( class57C.cvf )
