Cabview for UK Class86 Electric Locomotive.

Install:
 1 Backup and delete the oroginal folder of [CABVIEW].
 2 Unzip [86cabV1.zip] and put the folder of [CABVIEW]
   into your trainset(ex. [Virgin_86236]......).
 3 Backup and delete an original eng-file, and put eng-files of the
  folder of [Virgin_86236_eng-file] into your trainsets.

 * If you use another trainset, you must change next line of eng-file.
   CabView ( xxxx.cvf ) ----->  CabView ( class86.cvf )
   

-------------------------------------------------------------

   If you use common.cab

 1 Unzip [86cabV1.zip] and rename [CABVIEW] to [86cab].
 2 Put [86cab] into the folder of [common.cab].
 3 Put [class86C.cvf] into the folder of [CABVIEW] of your trainset(ex.[Virgin_86236]..).
 4 Backup and delete an original eng-file, and put all eng-files of the
  folder of [Virgin_86236C_eng-file] into your trainsets.
