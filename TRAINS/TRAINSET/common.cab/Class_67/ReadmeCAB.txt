Cabview for UK Class67 Diesel locomotive.

-------------------------------------------------------------
Install:
 1 Backup and delete the oroginal folder of [CABVIEW].
 2 Unzip [67cabV1.zip] and put the folder of [CABVIEW]
   into your trainset(ex.[67001][67002]...).
 3 Change next line of eng-file.

   CabView ( xxxx.cvf ) ----->  CabView ( class67.cvf )
   

-------------------------------------------------------------

   If you use common.cab

 1 Unzip [67cabV1.zip] and rename [CABVIEW] to [67cab].
 2 Put [67cab] into the folder of [common.cab].
 3 Put [class67C.cvf] into the folder of [CABVIEW] of your trainset.
 4 Change next line of eng-file.

   CabView ( xxxx.cvf ) ----->  CabView ( class67C.cvf )
