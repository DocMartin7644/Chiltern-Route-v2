Cabview for UK Class465(Networker) Electric Multiple Unit.

-------------------------------------------------------------
Install:
 1 Backup and delete an oroginal folder of [CABVIEW].
 2 Unzip [465cabV1.zip] and put the folder of [CABVIEW]
   into your trainset(ex.[ConnexClass465],[NSEClass465]...).
 3 Backup and delete an original eng-file, and put eng-files of the
  folder of [ConnexClass465] or [NSEClass465] into your trainsets.

 * If you use another trainset, you must change next line of eng-file.
   CabView ( xxxx.cvf ) ----->  CabView ( class465.cvf )
   

-------------------------------------------------------------

   If you use common.cab

 1 Unzip [465cabV1.zip] and rename [CABVIEW] to [465cab].
 2 Put [465cab] into the folder of [common.cab].
 3 Put [class465C.cvf] into the folder of [CABVIEW] of your trainset.
 4 Backup and delete an original eng-file, and put eng-files of the
  folder of [ConnexClass465] or [NSEClass465] into your trainsets.

 * If you use another trainset, you must change next line of eng-file.
   CabView ( xxxx.cvf ) ----->  CabView ( class465C.cvf )
