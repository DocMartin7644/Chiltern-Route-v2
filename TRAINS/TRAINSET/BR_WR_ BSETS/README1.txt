GWR BSET Non-Corridor Carrage All First. v3.0

3D Models built from scratch by Robin Howell (aeshop@dsl.pipex.com)
painted by Robin Howell
BR Crimson version painted by David Ward
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
The 3D model has been built using TrainSim Modeler v1.1

These Coaches were built in 1937 as part of a total of 84 vehicles. 

Versions in both BR Maroon and the earlier all over crimson livery are included.

Any suggestions of help or constructive comments welcome.
