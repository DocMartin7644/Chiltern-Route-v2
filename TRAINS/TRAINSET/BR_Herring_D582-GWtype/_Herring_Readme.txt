BR 25Ton Herring Ballast Hopper.
Version 1

Released : 16-11-2018

Model    : Ian Macmillan.
Textures : Ian Macmillan.

Designed by the GWR in 1947, as an upgrade to P7. 80 were built and allocated P22.
BR continued the build with 50 identical wagons to D582
All were unfitted. Some recieved vacuum or air through pipes but most were scrapped by the mid 80s.
Most allocated to GWR but some went to LMR and SR.

These wagons require Stuart Williamson's  SWB Wagon Sounds  File ID 3290
