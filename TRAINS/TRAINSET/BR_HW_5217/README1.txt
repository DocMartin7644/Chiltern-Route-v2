BR(W) 52XX 2-8-0T Locomotive  V1.0

3D Model built from scratch by Robin Howell (robin.howell@tiscali.co.uk)
painted by Robin Howell
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
The 3D model has been built using TrainSim Modeler

This loco represents 5217 in used condition in sometime in the 1950s.  They were mainly used on heavy mineral trains in south Wales but many saw service in the south west of England too.

Any suggestions of help or constructive comments welcome.
