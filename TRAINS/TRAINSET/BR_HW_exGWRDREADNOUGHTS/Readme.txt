Thank you for downloading these models
These three coaches include three coaches and three parcels/luggageVans all 70 footers. These are examples of  Churchwardd's earliest design. They were built during the period 1904-05, a number lasting into BR days. During their existence they were, typically used in formations that included varying types including the Toplights, Concertinas and later GWR stock.
